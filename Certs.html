Certifications

Objective

The goal of this page is to explain certifications. What are they, why are they important, and what kinds are there?

Certifications are credentials and commendations that individuals may achieve through testing of skills related to the specific certification. These certifications could be in many different areas of IT. They are essential because they show areas of expertise or elevated knowledge and skill. To enter the workforce, someone might want to have 1 or 2 certifications already acquired.

There are many different certifications that you can choose to work towards. These certifications may apply to different areas of IT, so it is important to find a certification that will apply to the job you want. Below are links to several sites that have information on different certifications.
