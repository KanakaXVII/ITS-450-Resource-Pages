Jobs in IT Compliance

Objective

The goal of this page is to provide some examples of jobs related to IT Compliance as well as to provide some links to useful job search websites.

IT security, more specifically compliance, are in very high demand. Jobs in compliance may be related to SOC (Security Operations Center), analyst, consultant, management, or other positions. Most jobs in compliance will involve managing business compliance rules and enforcing regulations to improve the security of the organization.


Job Tools

To begin finding a job in IT compliance, you must have some things to assist you. The first, and arguably most important, is a strong resume. A resume is often the first impression of you to potential jobs. A strong resume can make or break a job offer. Provided are several resources for creating a strong and effective resume.

  • How To Create a Professional Resume
  • Five Ways to Make Your Resume Ten Times Stronger
  • How To Create a Perfect Resume in 2019
  • Resume Tips
  • How To Write a Resume

The next important step in securing a job is creating a LinkedIn to serve as an online resume and portfolio. A LinkedIn profile should reflect all of your skills, experience, and relationships. Networking is a large part of finding a job. LinkedIn is a great way to create a network and make connections. Besides serving as an online resume, LinkedIn also provides a job search engine and various training modules for any topic.
